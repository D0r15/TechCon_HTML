History

Techcon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has since grown into one of the most influential technology conferences globally.

Originally focused on open-source development and software engineering, it quickly expanded to include topics like AI, cybersecurity, blockchain, and emerging tech

Over the years, TechCon has hosted thousands of professionals, launched countless startups, and served as a catalyst for innovation and collaboration in the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and inclusion in the global tech community.

Our mission is to empower professionals, inspire the next generation of creators, and spark transformative ideas by providing a platform where technology, creativity, and thought leadership converge.

Past Speakers

Below are a few past speakers:

Dr. Fei-Fei Li , a renowned computer scientist and AI pioneer, is a professor at Stanford University and co-director of the Stanford Human-Centered AI Institute. At TechCon, she delivered a powerful keynote on the ethical development of artificial intelligence and the importance of diversity in tech research.

Satya Nadella the CEO of Microsoft, spoke at TechCon about the future of cloud computing and AI-driven productivity. Under his leadership, Microsoft has transformed into a cloud-first company, and his insights on leadership and innovation were widely praised.

Elon Musk CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, captivated the audience with his vision of a multi-planetary future. His discussion on sustainable technology and space exploration inspired technologists to think beyond Earth and dream boldly.
